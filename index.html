Who exactly am I?

I’m a Systems design engineering student at the university of waterloo that’s passionate about design and the product lifecycle.

I like to think of myself as fluid: someone that can adapt to anything, and someone that loves spreading out to explore the new things. I come from a pretty diverse background that’s allowed me to a part of the complete product lifecycle.

I’m also a henna artist in my free time, and love doing art. If you want to get to know me further, feel free to keep scrolling, or reach out to me through one of the links below.
